App can be deleted by clicking the "Delete" button in the App detail page.

![App delete](/images/app-delete.png)

You will be presented with a confirmation page to delete the app.

![App delete confirmation](/images/app-delete-conf.png)

Once the app is deleted, you will be redirected to the App list page and get a confirmation message.

![App delete ack](/images/app-delete-ack.png)
