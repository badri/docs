A service can be deleted by clicking the "Delete" button in the service detail page.

![Delete Service](/images/delete-service.png)

You will get a confirmation form to delete the service,

![Delete Service confirmation](/images/svc-delete-conf.png)

and finally an acknowledgement saying that the service is deleted.

![Delete Service Ack](/images/svc-delete-ack.png)

The service and associated data will be completely removed from your cluster.

<Note>
It is important that the service is detached from all apps it is attached with before deleting, otherwise you won't be ablt to delete the service.
</Note>
