A service can be "detached" from an app. This involves removing the environment variable bindings from the app.

Go to the service detail page from the "Services" tab in the app,

![Services detail page](/images/service-tab.png)

In the Service details page, click on the "Detach" button next to the app you want to detach the service from.

![Services detach](/images/detach.png)

<Note>
This will trigger a redeployment of the app.
</Note>
